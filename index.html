A Man Born With A Golden Hand For India

General Manoj Mukund Naravane (born 22 April 1960) is an Indian Army General who is currently serving as the 27th Chief of the Army Staff (COAS), as well as the temporary Chairman of the Chiefs of Staff Committee since 15 December 2021.

Some Of The Achievements Of Manoj Mukund Narvane Sir

Personal Indo

Naravane hails from Pune, Maharashtra. He likes painting, yoga and gardening.

Army General

On 16 December 2019 (Vijay Diwas eve) he was announced as the successor of General Rawat

Career

Naravane was commissioned into 7th battalion The Sikh Light Infantry in June 1980.

Education

He also holds a master's degree in Defence Studies from University of Madras, Chennai and an M.Phil.
